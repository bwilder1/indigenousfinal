The Osage Nation In Missouri



Summary

Hello everyone and welcome to my website! My name is Ben Wilder, and I am a student at Babson College who recently has completed a course on Indigenous Peoples politics and history. As a final project, I have been tasked with creating a website to further educated people on the Osage Tribe in Missouri. Throughout the website you can learn about the Osage Tribe and the affects that settler colonialism has had on them. I want to make it clear that this website is not affiliated with the Osage Tribe, it is simply a way for me to express my learning through the form of a website.



Goal

The goal of my website is to educate people who are curious about the Osage Tribe in Missouri. I hope to use my education on the subject as a way to inspire people to support Indigenous communities that are local to them.
